Deep Dish but not in Chicago

Below is a list of ingredients needed to make a deep dish pizza. You can variate the type of ingredients for your own needs.

  • pizza dough
  • pizza sauce
  • fresh mozzarella
  • meats
    • pepperoni
    • sausage
    • ham
    • etc.
  • veggies
    • mushrooms
    • olives
    • etc.

You will also need a cast iron skillet. Preferably, 12" to 16" depending on how many people you want to feed.

You will also need a small amount of olive oil to coat the interior of the pan.

Recipe:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 425
  2. Prepare the skillet by using a light coating of olive oil around the sides and bottom of the skillet.
  3. Flatten the dough, place in skillet. be sure to leave a wall around the skillet at least an inch high.
  4. Place ingredients in skillet in the following order: cheese-toppings-sauce-extra cheese if you want
  5. When oven is ready, place skillet into oven for 25-30 minutes or until center is done.
  6. Allow pizza to cool after you take it out of the oven for at least five minutes.
  7. Slice, enjoy, repeat.
